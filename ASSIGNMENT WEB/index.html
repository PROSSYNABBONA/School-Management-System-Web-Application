Welcome to Bishop Cipriano Kihangire Secondary School

Established in 1985, Bishop Cipriano Kihangire Secondary School is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development. We nurture students to become responsible global citizens through our rigorous curriculum and diverse co-curricular programs. Our motto "Per Aspara Ad Astra" (Through Hardship to the Stars) reflects our commitment to helping students reach their highest potential.
